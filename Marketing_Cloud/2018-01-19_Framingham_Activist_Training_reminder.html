Heads up! There's a great event coming to Framingham tomorrow (January 20)!

We've all see images and videos of flooding in Boston. Now more than ever, what you and I do at the state and local level is crucial to mitigiate the effects of climate change!

We've held this training in over two dozen communities across the Commonwealth and are thrilled to bring it to Framingham tomorrow!

In this training, MA Sierra Club Executive Committee Member Deb Pasternak and Chapter Director Emily Norton will bring you up to speed on key energy bills under consideration at the State House and give you the tools you need to effectively advocate to your elected officials.

WHAT: Clean Energy Activist Training
WHEN: Tomorrow, Saturday, January 20, 2:00pm
WHERE: First Parish in Framingham, 24 Vernon St, Framingham, MA 01701

(Free and open to the public)

This event is co-sponsored by Transition Framingham, Sustainable Framingham, and First Parish Climate Action Team.
