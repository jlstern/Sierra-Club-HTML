This is a reminder about our Clean Energy Activist Training happening this Saturday in Jamaica Plain!

Big thank you to all our-cosponsors: Boston Climate Action Network; Mass Interfaith Power & Light; Boston Clean Energy Coalition (BCEC); JPNC Environment, Parks, and Energy Committee; JP Mothers Out Front; West Roxbury Saves Energy; and 350MA Boston Node.

In this training Joel Wool of Clean Water Action will bring you up to speed on key energy bills under consideration at the State House and Emily Norton, Sierra Club MA Chapter Director, will give you the tools you need to effectively advocate to your elected officials. There will also be a short presentation from Andy Bean of Boston Climate Action Network on what is already being done to engage locally on energy and climate issues in Boston.

RSVP: https://goo.gl/nofdNt (Free and open to the public)
WHEN: Saturday, February 17 at 2-4pm
WHERE: St. John's Episcopal Church, 1 Roanoke Ave, Jamaica Plain
(6 minutes walk from Green Street Orange Line stop)

Questions? Contact Jacob Stern at jacob.stern@sierraclub.org or 617-423-5775.
