Dear Framingham Friends,

The City We Want is hosting a Forum on Sustainability on Thursday 11/16 @ 6pm in the Blumer Room of the Memorial Building (150 Concord St Framingham MA, 01702). We hope that you can make it to this event to chat about how to make Framingham a more sustainable place to live, work and play.

This event is co-sponsored by two other volunteer organizations: Sustainable Framingham and Transition Framingham.

CLICK HERE TO RSVP

Topics for discussion:

  1. Solarize - Aimee Powelka will briefly explain the solarize program to open the forum to see what kind of interest there is for this kind of program in our community.
  2. Paris Climate Agreement - Larry Stoodt will give an overview of what other communities are doing to open the discussion of whether Framingham should consider making our own local commitments.
  3. Bikeability and Walkability - Ben Gustafson will give a brief overview of the 2017 Bicycle and Pedestrian Plan which should lead into a rich discussion on the topic.
  4. Sustainability Coordinator - We will wrap up the evening by talking about how the City of Framingham can help the planet as well as the tax payers by hiring for a position to focus on Sustainability and Energy Management.

If you have any questions, please contact Mike Croci (michaelbcroci@gmail.com) of Sustainable Framingham.

As always, thanks for all you do.

Jacob Stern
Clean Energy Organizer
