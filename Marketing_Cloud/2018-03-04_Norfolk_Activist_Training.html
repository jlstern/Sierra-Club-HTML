Heads up! The Massachusetts Sierra Club is hosting a clean energy activist training on March 13th in Norfolk!

We've all see images and videos of flooding in Boston. Now more than ever, what you and I do at the state and local level is crucial to mitigiate the effects of climate change! Just last Friday, Atlantic Ave in Boston flooded for the second time in two months. The time to act is now.

We've held this training in over two dozen communities across the Commonwealth and are thrilled to bring it to Norfolk later this month!

In this training, Sierra Club MA Chapter Director Emily Norton will bring you up to speed on key energy bills under consideration at the State House and give you the tools you need to effectively advocate to your elected officials.

WHAT: Clean Energy Activist Training
WHEN: Tuesday, March 13 from 7:00-8:30pm
WHERE: Norfolk Public Library, 139 Main St, Norfolk, MA 02056

(Free and open to the public)

This event is co-sponsored Action Together Massachusetts-Western Norfolk County.
