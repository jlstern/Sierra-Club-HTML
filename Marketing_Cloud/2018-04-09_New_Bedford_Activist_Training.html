Heads up! The Massachusetts Sierra Club is hosting a clean energy activist training on April 17th in New Bedford!

We've held this training in over two dozen communities across the Commonwealth and are thrilled to bring it to New Bedford later this month!

The fossil fuel industry has tremendous influence, even here in Massachusetts. Look no further than last weeks's Huffington Post article about Seekonk's own Rep. Howitt who was caught including a fossil fuel lobbyist on email replies to his own constituents. Your elected offcials need to hear from you!

In this training, Sierra Club MA Chapter Director Emily Norton and Executive Committee Chair Deb Pasternak will bring you up to speed on key energy bills under consideration at the State House and give you the tools you need to effectively advocate to your elected officials.

WHAT: Clean Energy Activist Training
WHEN: Tuesday, April 17 from 6:00-8:00pm
WHERE: Greasy Luck Brewpub, 791 Purchase St, New Bedford

(Free and open to the public, cash food and drink available)

This event is co-sponsored by Marching Forward, Marion Institute’s SouthCoast Energy Challenge, New Bedford Science Café, and UMass-Dartmouth’s Office of Campus Sustainability.
